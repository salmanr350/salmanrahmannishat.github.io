I am a Data Science master's student in the School of Mathematical and Statistical Science at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley, advised by Professor Tamer Oraby. Currently, I am working on the topics of computational social choices more specifically I am exploring the impact of cultural dimension, GDP, and human behavior on the COVID-19 pandemic integrating evolutionary game theory, and economic growth model.  

My research is centered on the field of data science and machine learning, more especially I am focusing on applied data science & machine learning, analytic, and network science. I am passionate about artificial intelligence, entrepreneurship, digital economy & new digital marketplaces, technology, and innovation.
